Our Process

Providing AI development services in healthcare can be a challenging endeavor without the right process. At Apro Software, we’ve developed a streamlined system for turning your ideas into reality.

Interview

All successful projects start with getting to know you, your business, and your customers. We use this intel to understand the problems you face, the features that you need, and the AI outcomes you want to achieve.

Plan

After the interview, we analyze the information we gathered and start to suggest design concepts based on you and your customer’s needs. Documenting everything from hardware requirements to project schedules in the process. Then we choose the solution you want to develop.

Develop

In the development phase, we create the first version of the full product. Our team works together behind-the-scenes in two-week sprints to develop the solution. At the end of each sprint, we invite you to review the progress we’ve made. (Or you can attend our daily meetings if you want real-time updates.)

Support

Delivering the healthcare AI solution isn’t the end of the journey. We’ll continue to work with you and make updates and changes as required. If you decide to make changes to the system’s environment, we can help with that as well.
